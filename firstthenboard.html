Our Why

Started at Home. Built for Every Family.

As a parent, I know what it feels like to search for a tool that actually fits your child's world. When my daughter with autism began navigating daily routines, I needed something visual, simple, and always within reach — so I built it.

First Then Board started as a personal project: a way to show her what comes first and what comes next using her own photos. No overwhelming menus. No complicated setup. Just a clear, calm picture of what to expect.

What began for one family has grown into a tool for parents, teachers, therapists, and caregivers everywhere who believe visual structure can change everything.

"Visual clarity isn't a workaround — it's a bridge."

Features

Everything Your Family Needs. Nothing Extra.

📷

Your Photos, Your Child's World

Use photos from your camera or library — or any visual supports you already use. Add text labels under each image. Pinch to zoom and reposition. Drag to swap panels instantly. Boards that feel familiar to your child.

📋

Multi-Step Routines

Go beyond First and Then. Add up to 5 middle steps for morning routines, bedtime sequences, and any multi-step activity. Switch between vertical or side-by-side layouts to match your child's preference.

📊

Progress Logging & Reports

Log each completed routine with a tap — or hold to note when more time was needed. No judgment, just data. Export a full history to CSV for IEP updates and care team meetings.

⏱️

Quick Boards & Auto-Scheduling

Save your most-used routines and schedule them to load automatically by day and time. Morning routine at 7am, every day — set it once and let the app handle the rest.

👨‍👩‍👧

Multiple Child Profiles

Manage each child separately with their own boards, saved photos, and completion history. Per-child PIN protection keeps each profile private. Built for families who support more than one.

📶

Always Available

Works completely offline — no internet required. Screen lock mode keeps the board front and center when you need it most. Available in English and Spanish.
