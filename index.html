Our Why

A Communication Tool Born at Home

Every parent wants to help their child thrive. When our daughter began her journey through ABA therapy and speech, we searched for a tool that could meet her where she was — something visual, simple, and always within reach.

First Then Board started as a personal project: a digital PECS-style board that shows a child what comes first and what comes next. No overwhelming menus. No complicated setup. Just a clear, calm picture of what to expect.

What began as a tool for one family is now available to every family, every therapist, and every classroom that believes visual communication is a superpower — not a limitation.

"Visual clarity isn't a workaround — it's a bridge."

Features

Everything You Need. Nothing You Don't.

📋

Visual First/Then Boards

Create clear, simple visual schedules that reduce anxiety and help children understand what's coming next. Less guessing. More trust.

🖼️

PECS-Style Card Library

A built-in library of picture exchange communication cards covering daily routines, activities, emotions, and more — ready to use out of the box.

⏱️

Quick Boards & Scheduling

Set up boards in advance and schedule them to appear automatically at the right time of day. Your child's routine, on autopilot.

📷

Custom Cards

Add your own photos and labels to make boards personal. Use real images from your child's world for maximum understanding and engagement.

📶

Works Offline

No internet? No problem. First Then Board works completely offline — so it's always available when and where you need it most.

🧠

Built for ABA & Speech

Designed with the structure therapists rely on and the simplicity families need — a tool that bridges home, school, and the therapy room.
