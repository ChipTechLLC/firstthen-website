Who We Are

Founded on Purpose

ChipTech LLC was born out of a personal need — the kind that only a parent truly understands. Our first product, First Then Board, was created to support our daughter's communication journey through ABA therapy and speech. It grew into something larger: proof that purposeful technology can make a real difference in people's lives.

We're a small company with a big commitment — to build software that's simple, intentional, and genuinely useful. For families. For professionals. For anyone who needs a tool that just works.
