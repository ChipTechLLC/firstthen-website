Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 27, 2025

1. Introduction

ChipTech LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the First Then Board mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the practices described here.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Custom cards and content: Photos, labels, and board configurations you create within the App are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or access this content.
  • Contact information: If you contact us for support, we may collect your name and email address solely to respond to your inquiry.

Automatically Collected Information

  • App usage data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage data (such as feature usage frequency and crash reports) to improve the App. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.
  • Device information: We may collect non-identifying device information such as operating system version and device model for diagnostic purposes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information through normal App use.
  • We do not collect information about children beyond what is stored locally on the device by the user.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not sell your data to third parties — ever.

3. Children's Privacy

First Then Board is designed to be used by parents, caregivers, and therapists on behalf of children. We are committed to complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • All content created within the App (photos, boards, cards) is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us at support@chiptechllc.com and we will promptly delete it.

4. How We Use Information

Any information we collect is used solely to:

  • Operate and improve the App
  • Respond to support requests
  • Diagnose technical issues and crashes
  • Understand how features are used (in aggregate, never individually)

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond what is listed above.

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local storage: All boards, cards, and custom content are stored on your device only. We do not have access to this data.
  • Cache: The App may cache PECS category and card data locally on your device to improve performance. This data is not tied to your identity.
  • Security: We use industry-standard practices to protect any data transmitted to or from our systems. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Third-Party Services

The App may use the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — for app distribution and in-app purchase processing
  • Apple / Google — for crash reporting and performance monitoring (if enabled on your device)

We do not integrate third-party advertising SDKs or social media tracking tools.

7. In-App Purchases

Premium features are available through in-app purchases processed by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not collect or store your payment information. All billing is handled by Apple or Google under their respective terms and privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@chiptechllc.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
