Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2025  ·  Last Updated: April 3, 2026

ChipTech LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the First Then Board mobile application (the "App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal data through normal use of the App. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or any account information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data of any kind
  • Photos, boards, or any content you create in the App

All content you create — boards, cards, photos, child profiles, and completion history — is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

Support Communications

If you contact us for support (e.g., by emailing support@chiptechllc.com), we may receive your name and email address. This information is used solely to respond to your inquiry and is not stored beyond what is necessary to resolve it.

2. Children's Privacy

First Then Board is designed for use by parents, guardians, caregivers, and therapists on behalf of children. We take children's privacy seriously.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • All content created within the App — including photos, boards, and child profiles — is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.
  • We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • If you believe we have inadvertently received information from or about a child, please contact us at support@chiptechllc.com and we will address it promptly.

3. Third-Party Services

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store. Apple may collect certain information in connection with app distribution, in-app purchases, and crash reporting under their own privacy policies. We do not control Apple's data practices.

We do not integrate any third-party advertising networks, analytics SDKs, or social media tracking tools.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive, collect, or store any payment or billing information.

4. Advertising

The free version of the App displays ads served by Apple's advertising framework (Apple Ads). Apple manages this independently under their own privacy policy. We do not receive personal data from Apple in connection with ad delivery. Upgrading to Premium removes all ads.

5. Data Security

Because we do not collect or store personal data on our systems, the risk of a data breach involving your personal information from our side is effectively zero. All App data lives on your device, protected by your device's own security (passcode, Face ID, etc.).

6. Your Rights

Because we do not collect or retain personal data, there is generally nothing for us to access, correct, or delete on your behalf. If you have contacted us for support and wish to have that communication deleted, you may request it at support@chiptechllc.com.

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). Given that we do not collect personal information, there is no data to disclose, delete, or opt out of selling. We do not sell personal information — ever.

7. Data Retention

We do not retain user data on our servers. All App data is stored locally on your device and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing App data through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Any changes will be noted in the App's release notes and go into effect with the next app update. Continued use of the App after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
